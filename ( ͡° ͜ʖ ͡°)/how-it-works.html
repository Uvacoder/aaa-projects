How does one join the AppSeed network?

We pride ourselves with the individuals on our platform. At the moment, AppSeed accepts applicants on a very limited basis. All of our applicants must go through an interview process to ensure that they'd be the right fit for every employer on our platform that they could be potentially applying for. In order to become apart of AppSeed's network, click here to be taken to the application.



What is the application process?

To make sure all of our applicants meet our requirements, each applicant will have to go through a multistage screening process. You will go through the following phases in your journey to being apart of the AppSeed network:

  • Language proficiency & communication - The language proficency and communication exam will be started once the applicant responds to our communication requests after they've applied. This is exam measures the applicant's English proficiency in reading comprehension, writing, and communication.
  • Skill & Knowledge Review - Applicants will next begin taking various assessments based on the career path they're looking to pursue. Each individual exam is designed to reward creativity and innovation. Each type of exam is then graded by an industry veteran with advanced knowledge in the specific field the applicant is taking assessments in.
  • Live interview - If the applicant has made it this far, they will next begin an interview with a representative where they will be judged based on professionalism, personality, communication skills, and expertise. They will then conduct live assessments with the representative to test knowledge.
  • Final project - Based on the applicant's field of study, they will be given a final project that is decided from evaluating their skillset, knowledge, personality, communication skills, and work ethic. The projects will then be reviewed by an expertise who will grade them out of 100. If they recieve a 80% or higher they will be allowed on the platform and can begin applying for jobs.


What are the requirements to use AppSeed?

In order to preserve absolute quality in our candidates, we ask that all applicants are at least 16 years of age and are authorized to work in the United States. There may be other requirements per the discretion of each individual opportunity.



How long does it take to get approved as an applicant?

We will call, text, or email you within 72 hours of your submission of your application. It may take up to 30 days to get fully accepted into the program.



Does it cost anything to use AppSeed as an applicant?

No. AppSeed is completely free to applicants.
